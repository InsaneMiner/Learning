About Learning

Learning is a completely free and GNU licensed LMS (learning management system) platform, which is aimed to help people to learn and teach about everything they think would be useful for someone else.

Learning has passed through 2 phases, which are basically the same concept in a less elaborated way, (the only one that is still alive is H4CK1NGB00TC4MP)

You can create, modify and delete the source of Learning. You can even create your own Learning-clone with customised courses, different blog entries and even footer links and design, but before you do that, please read first The License
